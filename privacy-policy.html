Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to Yario. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create a Yario account, we collect your email address, username, and profile information you choose to provide.

Chat Data: Messages exchanged with the Yario AI are stored to provide conversation continuity and improve your experience.

Device Information: We collect device ID, IP address, operating system version, and app version for analytics and security purposes.

Camera and Microphone: Used only during video and voice calls with your explicit consent. Yario does not record or store these calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Yario service
  • To improve and personalise your experience
  • To improve our AI models and features
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To communicate with you about your account or updates
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Yario uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication
  • DeepInfra — AI inference and processing
  • LiveKit — real-time video and voice calls

Your data is processed securely through these services in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your Yario account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Yario app settings. Upon deletion, your data is removed within 30 days.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption.

6. Children's Privacy

Yario is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data Yario holds about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Yario after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us
